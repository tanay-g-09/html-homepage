Passed away on January 26, 2020

died on January 26, 2020, due to blunt force trauma sustained in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California. The crash occurred during dense fog, which severely reduced visibility. Investigations concluded that the pilot likely became spatially disoriented, causing the helicopter to descend rapidly and collide with a hillside. There was no evidence of mechanical failure, and all nine people on board, including Kobe Bryant and his daughter Gianna Bryant, were killed instantly.
